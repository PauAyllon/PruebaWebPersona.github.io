ABOUT ME

I am Mónica Cases, a Spanish junior concept artist and illustrator, also known as Fungi_crow on social media.

I'm studying the 4th course of BBAA at the Polytechnic University of Valencia. I am mainly dedicated to character design, props and colour scripts.

Since I was little, I have been fascinated by the world of animation and video games, and I would love to be part of that beautiful world.
